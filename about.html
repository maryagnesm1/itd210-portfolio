My Creative Journey

Hello, my name is Mary Mazujian. I am an art enthusiast who is rediscovering her passion for creative expression and love of art history.

Things that inspire me

There are so many things that can inspire creativity. A simple walk outdoors and seeing nature. No matter how many times you see the sunset it is still magnificent. Learning to notice and appreciate those little things that we take for granted helps maintain a happy content life.
